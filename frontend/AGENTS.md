# Development Guide
1. Place the page/layout-specific assets, components, hooks, and actions on _assets/, _components/, _hooks/, and _actions/ folders in level with the page.tsx/layout.tsx on app router.
2. Place the shared assets, components, hooks, and actions (even only for 2 pages) on src/shared/assets/, src/shared/components/, src/shared/hooks/, and src/shared/actions/ folders.
3. Assets are the svg, png, or any assets.
